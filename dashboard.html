How to Find Your Form ID

  1. Open your Google Form in a web browser
  2. Look at the URL in the address bar, which will look something like:
    https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx/viewform
  3. The Form ID is the long string between /e/ and /viewform
  4. Copy this ID and paste it into the appropriate field above
