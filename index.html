How It Works

  1. Login with your Google account to securely access your forms
  2. Enter the IDs of your three language-specific forms (English, Spanish, Polish)
  3. Our tool retrieves all responses and translates non-English content
  4. Responses are combined into a unified dataset for comprehensive analysis
  5. Visualizations and insights are automatically generated
